FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How exactly does "pay for results" work?+

During the free assessment, we work with you to set a measurable outcome benchmark — such as customer-service response hours, quote turnaround speed, conversion rate or error rate. Once the system is live and hits the agreed benchmark, we charge on a revenue-share or performance basis. If we miss the target, this stage is free.

Is it really completely free upfront?+

Yes. There are no consulting fees for the assessment or the first deployment — we take the risk on ourselves. Billing starts only after the AI is actually live and delivering the agreed results.

Do we need to organize our data first?+

No. Our deployment process includes data inventory and cleanup. You just provide your existing systems and files, and we handle the rest of the integration work.

We're a traditional industry — does this apply to us?+

Absolutely. We focus on front-line scenarios for Taiwan's SMBs — with real-world cases in manufacturing, trading and services. The more repetitive and labor-intensive the process, the more results you'll see.

Will we be locked into a specific cloud or tool?+

No. We integrate primarily with your existing systems and choose the most suitable, maintainable option for each scenario, avoiding unnecessary lock-in and extra cost.
