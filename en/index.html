FAQ

Frequently asked

How is an Agent different from a regular chatbot?+

A chatbot answers one question at a time; an Agent proactively breaks down the task, calls tools and systems, checks its own results and corrects them until the task is done — only asking for your confirmation at key points.

Do we need to clean up our data first?+

No. Our adoption process itself includes data inventory and cleanup. You just provide your existing systems and files; we handle the integration work.

Will we be locked into a specific cloud or tool?+

No. We integrate around your existing systems and pick the most suitable, maintainable option per scenario, avoiding unnecessary lock-in and cost.

How is data security and privacy protected?+

We can use private deployment and in-house RAG retrieval, keeping sensitive data 100% internal, with access controls and audit logs in place.

We're a traditional industry — does this apply to us?+

Absolutely. The more repetitive and labor-intensive the process, the bigger the impact — we have real cases in manufacturing, trade, retail and logistics.
