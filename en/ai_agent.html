FAQ

What owners ask most

Will adoption be expensive and take forever?+

No. We start with a single NT$12,000 process clinic to pinpoint the steps most worth doing; a POC starts from NT$60,000 and can go live in as little as a week. Validate the value at low cost first, then decide whether to scale.

Will AI replace my employees?+

Our goal is to free your staff from repetitive, tedious work so they can do higher-value things — serving loyal customers, handling exceptions, building relationships. The AI takes on the parts people don't want to do.

We're not technical at all — is that okay?+

Absolutely. We handle the technical side; you just need to know your business. We communicate in language you understand and provide staff onboarding training.

Do we need to organize our data before starting?+

No. Data inventory and cleanup are part of the adoption process. You just provide your existing systems and files, and we take care of the rest.

Is our data safe? Could it leak?+

We can use private deployment and in-house retrieval, keeping sensitive data 100% inside your company, with access controls and audit logs in place.

How soon will we see results?+

You'll see the first number on real traffic during the POC stage (about 1-2 weeks), with a typical payback period of 3-6 months.
