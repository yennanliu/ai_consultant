# AI Consultant Blog Creation Skill

## Purpose
Create professional, data-driven blog posts for the AI consultant website targeting Taiwan SMEs. All posts follow the same design system, professional tone, and content structure.

## Target Audience
- Taiwan small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs)
- Business decision-makers and technical leaders
- Organizations exploring AI adoption strategies

## Professional Tone & Voice
- Business-focused with practical emphasis
- Balance between strategic vision and operational reality
- Provide specific metrics, timelines, and ROI data
- Avoid jargon; explain technical concepts clearly
- Frame recommendations as best practices proven in real cases
- Taiwan-specific context and industry references

## Blog Post Structure

### Content Organization (Required)
1. **Opening Paragraph** — Context + Market data/statistic
2. **2-3 Main Sections** — Numbered headers with breakdown of key concepts
3. **Data & Examples** — Specific metrics, timelines, case studies
4. **Frameworks/Processes** — Structured approaches or step-by-step methods
5. **Key Takeaways** — 3-5 actionable principles or success factors

### Content Standards

**Statistics & Data:**
- Include 3-5 quantified metrics (market size, adoption rates, efficiency gains, ROI, timelines)
- Source where possible (Gartner, IDC, Taiwan industry reports, observed case data)
- Use data to support claims, not decorate them
- Format: percentage gains, cost savings, time reductions, or error rates

**Frameworks & Processes:**
- Provide structured breakdowns of complex topics
- Use numbered phases, bullet points, or decision trees
- Include timelines for multi-phase initiatives (e.g., "4-8 weeks for Phase 1")
- Show decision logic or trade-offs when relevant

**Case Studies:**
- Real or realistic examples with specific numbers
- Show problem → solution → outcome arc
- Include financial metrics (investment, savings, ROI, payback period)
- Highlight non-financial benefits (brand, capability, scalability)

**Professional Depth:**
- Technical posts should explain "why" not just "what"
- Business posts should address organizational and change management aspects
- Always include risk mitigation or common pitfalls to avoid

## Key Content Patterns to Follow

### Pattern 1: Opening with Data
```
"隨著...（趨勢），2026 年將...根據 [Source] 報告，[具體數字]..."
```
Example: "根據 Gartner 與 IDC 2026 AI 展望報告，全球 AI 市場規模已達 1,850 億美元，年增長率達 34%。"

### Pattern 2: Structured Breakdowns
```
<strong>關鍵 [Framework/Point]：</strong>
• Bullet 1 — [Specific metric/benefit]
• Bullet 2 — [Specific metric/benefit]
• Bullet 3 — [Specific metric/benefit]
```

### Pattern 3: Process Timelines
```
<strong>階段 N：[Name]（[Duration]）</strong>
[Description of activities and expected outcomes]
```
Example: "週 1-2：資料收集與標註 — 蒐集 5,000 張樣本圖像..."

### Pattern 4: Economic Impact
```
<strong>經濟效益（[Period]）：</strong>
• 投資：[Amount] 台幣
• 成本節省：[Amount] 台幣/[period]
• 收入增長：[Amount] 台幣/[period]
• ROI / 回收期：[Metric]
```

### Pattern 5: Key Success Factors
```
<strong>[Number]. [Factor Title]</strong> — [Explanation with specific impact]
```

## Language & Formatting Rules

**Traditional Chinese (繁體中文):**
- All content in zh-Hant
- Use proper Chinese punctuation (。，、；：「」)
- Currency: Taiwan Dollar with TWD or 台幣 notation
- Time periods: 2026 年, 3-6 個月, etc.

**Emphasis & Structure:**
- Use `<strong>Title</strong>` for key concepts and section titles within content
- Use `<h2>Header</h2>` for major section divisions only
- Use `<br/>` within `<p>` tags for bullet lists (see Pattern 2 format above)
- Keep paragraphs focused; max 3-4 sentences before a list or new section

**Links in Content:**
- Internal links to other posts: `<a href="[slug].html">[text]</a>`
- Back link at bottom: `<a href="../blog.html" class="back-link">← BACK TO BLOG</a>`

## Dataset & Reference Standards

### Required Elements per Post Type

**AI Trends / Market Analysis:**
- 2-3 market statistics (market size, growth rate, adoption rate)
- 3+ industry-specific insights (Taiwan context preferred)
- 3+ future trends or opportunities
- Forward-looking timeline (e.g., "by 2026", "next 18 months")

**Technical Guides:**
- Architecture layers or decision framework (3-5 components)
- 3+ design decisions with trade-offs explained
- 2-3 implementation considerations
- Cost/complexity assessment

**Case Studies:**
- Company profile (industry, size, revenue if possible)
- Initial problem statement with quantified impact (error rate, cost, time)
- Solution approach with technical details
- Implementation timeline (phases, duration)
- Results with financial metrics (investment, ROI, payback)
- Non-financial benefits (capability, competitive advantage, team transformation)
- 5+ key success factors or lessons learned

**Strategy Guides:**
- 4-5 step framework with durations
- 3+ critical success criteria or decision points
- 4+ common pitfalls with mitigation strategies
- Resource requirements (team, budget, skills)
- Measurable outcomes or KPI framework
- 3+ actionable principles or best practices

**Implementation/Governance:**
- 4+ phases with timelines and activities
- Decision rights and responsibility matrix
- Governance framework with 4+ pillars
- Risk/mitigation pairs
- Team roles and capabilities needed

## File Management

**Storage Location:** `/asset/[filename].html`

**Naming Convention:**
- Kebab-case: lowercase, hyphens between words
- Descriptive: `enterprise-private-agent-architecture.html`
- Avoid: abbreviations unless industry-standard (e.g., "ROI", "POC")

**Version Control:**
- Commit new posts with descriptive message
- Include blog.html update in same commit
- Tag release milestones (e.g., "v1.0-launch", "blog-q2-2026")
